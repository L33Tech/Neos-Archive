Hello😊, I can see there are a few questions regarding the mechanism of Cooper's Planet. So I will try to clarify..

The maximum staking percentage is 20% pa. That is if you reach the 'Multiverse' rank. For more details on the ranking system, please refer to the whitepaper: https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/61e55a05ff9ce033ad45f7fa/64dafa270d0e402b8fe044e6_Neos%20Whitepaper%201.10.pdf

As for the progression, the system is designed for rapid progression at the beginning, which slows down as you approach the higher tiers (There are 3 tiers - Blue, Red and Gold).

There's a daily limit of 4 meteors that can be mined, and each meteor contains between 1 to 5 Stardust nuggets that you can collect. This means you'll get a minimum of 4 and a maximum of 20 nuggets per day. Please just bear with us as there might be some inconsistencies in the meteor count in this preview version.

As well as issues with Stardust tank filling - when tank is full, and all Stardust is collected for the day, you should receive auditory feedback indicating that the daily limit of Stardust collection has been reached. This cue is currently not in place.
However once you've collected all nuggets, the gun will no longer break down meteors. So no need to do anything else, you can just return next day and collect more. ☺️
We're working on improvements and fixes where possible.

And then there's eventually going to be the mentioned Unreal/Web version which we have in development, where you will be able to do the same. Just without having to launch the whole NeosVR.
